Clinical Technology

Sees What NEWS2 Can't — Until It's Too Late

Traditional scoring waits for thresholds to be crossed. Our AI reads the trajectory of change in real time.

  • 📈
    Rolling Trend Detection
    10-minute rolling means and standard deviations for all vitals capture drift before any single reading crosses a threshold.
  • 💡
    Shock Index & Composite Markers
    Clinically validated composite features including Shock Index (HR/SBP) and Respiratory Distress Index augment raw readings.
  • 🕐
    Tiered Alert Architecture
    AI fires a soft Tier-1 watch alert. NEWS2 remains as the hard Tier-2 escalation. Reduced fatigue, zero missed events.
  • 🔒
    GDPR & NHS Data Security
    On-premise deployment options, IG Toolkit compliant, no patient data leaves your trust's network without consent.
